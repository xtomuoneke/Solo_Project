About doyenTech

doyenTech is an Adaptive E-learning Platform

doyenTech is designed to train and produce distinguished personnels both in programming and other fields of IT and education. Its main feature is to provide customized training leveraging on deliberate practice principles to bring out the expertise in you.

We seek to solve the problem of mediocrity and to overcome the enemy called average which are now almost ubiquitos in our society. Our system is designed to address the issue of low human capital development and wastage of time and resources that hamper greatly the development of any society. The ugly issue of mass dropout in trainings is also one of our targets in this system.
